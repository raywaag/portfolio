About

With a mission to heal, distract and get a few gnarly laughs, The Happiness Project is an atmosphere to find humor, journaling, stories, tips and distractions.


I’ve personally payed debt to my demons and wish for others to seek peace in those times. My vision was an environment where people can comfortably exist without judegment or toxic loopholes like social media. Play games, journal, talk to others, seek help and find comfort. That’s what The Happiness Project is about.


We have all had nights, days, weeks or months that drown us in darkness. The Happiness Project is for those who are unable to itch or cope their way to contentment.

The Challenge

    There were many thought-worthy challenges in taking on a heavy subject like mental health.

  • Fragile users. The Happiness Project is targeted towards a sensitive audience. Whether they are just taking a break from reality or urgently seeking help, the options need to be readily available.
  • Coping possibilities. Users come in all different mindsets. Some may cope by journaling while others by gaming or seeking a good laugh. This means that THP needed to adderess a variety of healthing coping alternatives for its audience.
  • Censorship with freedoms. Within HTP there is abaility to write and post stories or jokes on the feed. This leads to great opportunity to post inappropriate content, especailly when catering to a younger audience.
  • Liability. A huge challenge not only in design but in legality. We want the user to feel comfortable and safe in the THP environment.

Development Factors

    A hierarchical task analysis was performed on competitor, Care.com, to better understand the alterations that needed to be made or features to include in order for ShutterSpeed to reach its utmost success.

  • Account creation. Since THP has features that are benefit the user when retained, such as mood tracking and journaling, through PHP the user can create an account and then use that to log in and out of the site.
  • Mood tracking. Under the navigation, you can find "Today's Mood" which allows the tracking of your mood each day. After the user tracks their mood, they are presented with an according message and given coping suggestions such as games, articles or access to hotlines.
  • Say Something. The "Say Something" tab gives users a chance to share funny or inspiring stories with the community. Stored in the database, a user can write, title and date their entry followed by past entries.
  • Contact. THP is first and foremost for the user. A contact section is provided to recieve questions, comments, feedback or stories from out users.
