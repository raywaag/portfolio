Paws With a Purpose

Roles: UX Research, Wireframes, IA, Mock-ups and Interaction Design

ABOUT

Second place winner of Adobe Creative Jam Fall 2018. Paws for a Purpose is an app created for veterans used to help find a service dog suitable to their needs. Every veteran has their own story and we may never be able to understand what they are going through due to what they’ve been through. What we do know, however, is that we have the opportunity to help and we will do everything in our power to make the lives of our veterans the best we possibly can.

THE PROBLEM

When a veteran is in need of a service dog, they may not know exactly where to start. There are many service dog organizations available and it can tend to be overwhelming. By going through Paws for a Purpose, veterans are able to see multiple well-known options in a single location, compare, and ultimately make the best decision for their life and the life of their future companion.

AUDIENCE DEMOGRAPHICS

  • Veterans who...
  • - are in need of a service dog
  • - have a disability that permits them to a service animal
  • - want a simple, easy way to find an organization that is best for them
  • - wish to read reviews of organizations to find the best fit for them
  • - seek the contact information of organizations
  • - want a confident experience in finding their fur-ever companion

USERS

  • Retired Veterans
  • Consider the following possible diabilities:
  • Deafness
  • PTSD
  • Loss or lack of site
  • Age - 25+ years; majority over 35 years

STAKEHOLDERS

  • Service Dog Organizations
  • Veterans
  • Service Dog Trainers/Raisers
  • Loved ones of veterans
