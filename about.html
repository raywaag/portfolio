About

Hi, I'm Rachel.

Captivated by design and its impact on everyday life, I strive to pull the human back into HCI and user-centered design practices. To me, the beauty of the UX field is the never-ending ingression of knowledge. My passion to learn has been a beneficial guide to resolving digital problems and producing optimal and effective user experiences.

With studies in Web Development and Human Factors Psychology under my belt, I am able to communicate and provide quality deliverables that're advantageous to all teams throughout the UX process. I've studied, researched and designed for many different environments including Responsive Web, iOS and 10-foot views (TV).

While I am abundantly passionate about my everyday UX tasks, you can also find me enjoying the outdoors with my dogs, Jolene and Finn, and Nikon camera.
