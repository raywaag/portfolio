About

A collection of a southern family's distillery, Skeet Shot Distillery. This site is a residence of their crafts, recipes, family biographies and communication.


The Skeet Shot site is still under development and the mobile version is currently undergoing its interaction design process. Check back soon to see Skeet Shot Distillery's progress!

Research

From being unfamiliar with distilleries in general to creating a site accomodating to all user querries, I heavily conducted research on current distillery sites such as some of the greats like Jack Daniel's and the locals like Glacier Distillery. Using the current marlet as a template, I was able to create a sitemap to successfully portray the Skeet Shot family, business and friendliness.





Current Distillery sites, Jack Daniel's and Glacier Distilling.

Design

When it came to the palette of the site, it was ideal that the text, navigation and footer all be a slightly muted black. Even accents like headers better presented in black or white text. This allowed me to display images throughout the site without it competing with the site colors.

When creating the logo for Skeet Shot, the Reeders expressed the necessity of a primarily black logo. Understanding the darkened theme of many distillery and alcoholic enironments, creating a palette to match that was no question.



User Flow

User profile: 33-year old, Bryan, looking to conjure a recipe using his Skeet Shot Whiskey.

Boards Needed

  • Home: Summarize the Reeders and the Skeet Shot Distillery. This page will have many paths stemming from it, allowing the user to go anywhere on the site from this one page.
  • Story: Express to the user the story of the businees answering who the Reeders are (and who Skeet was), why "Skeet Shot" & who a distillery and where the magic happens.
  • Products: Display the variety of products available from the distillery. Connect this page with the drink's detai page.
  • Product detail: When the user selects a product from the product page they will be taken to this page, engaging them in an all-inclusive look of the prodoct and its possbibilities.
  • Recipes: A list of reknowned recipes from Skeet Shot spirits with the ability to filter results by product, occassion and taste.
  • Contact: A classic contact page with a form and Google Map API of the disitllery's location as well as the outputted response of a successfully submitted form.


Skeet Shot Walkthrough
