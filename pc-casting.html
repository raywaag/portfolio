P & C Casting

What
Paws & Claws Casting (P & C Casting) is a site for potential actors with amenities such as casting call postingtalent search and features of the world’s next Rin Tin Tin all in the name of animals. At P & C Casting, users can write reviews for actors, search fo the next star, post casting calls and create accounts & profile with biographies, ratings, past achievments and photo galleries.
Why
We fanaticize about movie stars but a quick look on the ‘Gram will show you that the world is just as fascinated with the furry, scaly and feathery infamous celebrities. P & C Casting is for them. To enrich the discovery of newly found animal talents and ease the process of talent-finding for the agents.
Who
Animal actors stretching internationally who are in need of work and want to display their profiles, achievements and talents. Humans are easy but animals are the future. Find the next Airbud, Lassie or Trigger. P & C Castjng is for the geniuses on and off the screen. Talent agents can find their next big star while the actors can promote themselves with confidence.

My Roles

Project Manager, Interface Design and Front-End Development & Back-End Devlopment

Workflow

Research

Upon competitor research, it was found that the current market is dated, underworked and essentially of little threat. Little thought goes into the interface design of many websites, giving P & C a pruposeful design advantage.

Design

P & C's design is influenced by the film and cinema industry of gold, silver and black. The gold and yellows accetned through out the site to represent significance while black, silver and white made for a distinguishable, professional sitemap.
We wanted to limit user clicks to save time and retention which lead to our landing page hosting much of the site's content from current stars to casting calls.

Develop

Development of P & C had a base of HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript and functional with PHP and MySQL.
