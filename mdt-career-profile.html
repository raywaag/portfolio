Employee Career Journey, Discovery, & Management

At the core of a MyDisneyToday employee profile was a job portal that hosted internal HR workflows enabling employees to explore internal career opportunities, apply to open positions, and track their career development track.

Overview

MyDisneyToday's flexibility and customization offered a chance to incorporate HR products into the platform experience and further streamline employee needs, goals, and connection. This prototype became a concept design distributed to leadership across multiple departments.

Pain Points

Job-hunting, networking, and professional development affected internal mobility within Disney due to the following glaring issues:

  • Multiple Touchpoints - Employees are required to visit multiple platforms and portals to search, apply, and check on jobs.
  • Poor Communication - Reaching out to recruiters, teams, and hiring managers can be undependable via email alone.
  • Redundant Application Steps - Filling out the same information for every application can become repetitive and tiresome.
  • Unintuitive Search - Searching for roles delivers a variety of results, some less relevant than others.
  • Inconsistent Job Details - Some roles provide more details than others including the hiring manager and team.
  • Unclear Status - After applying, candidates struggle to follow the ambiguous hire timeline.
