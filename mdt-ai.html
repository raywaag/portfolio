Functionality

Navigating Internal Job Boards

Finding an internal role at Disney is intimidating, overwhelming, and time-consuming. Using AI, we could make the job search seamless, improves the accuracy of results, save employees time, and increase productivity while supportingcinternal mobility.

Cover Letters & Interview Prep

Preparing for interviews is daunting, disconcerting, and unnerving. AI can support employee preparedness by giving them confidence and providing evidence-based advice to increase the liklihood of applicant success.

Professional Development

Professional development and learning at Disney can be tedious, unproductive, uncertain, and inaccurate. Resources can often seem basic or too generalized. Combining AI and development, we could suggest relative courses and certifications and improve accuracy of education received by understanding the employee’s present and future career goals.

Networking & Connecting

Networking at Disney was nearly impossbile. It's an intimidating tasked and often goes underutilized. AI could encourage connection, match employees across the company, and raise awareness for the importance of networking and connections.
