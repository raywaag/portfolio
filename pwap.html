About

Paws With A Purpose serves as a third party networking app to discover organizations that offer veterans service dogs with ease, simplicity and credibility.


The process of searching, discovering and reaching out to a service dog organization can be a complex, broad and uneasy journey. Paws With A Purpose is available to create a friendly, navigable environment for veterans to better understand and find organizations that are most suitable to them and their disabilities.


Paws With a Purpose is for approved veterans seeking their fur-ever companion. Age range varies with a mjority above 35 years.

Research

Research was heavily invested in service dog organizations and their specifics to better customize PWAP. This allowed the team to develop pathways specialized to the user's disabilty type. Our biggest market competitor is NAGDU Guide & Service Dog Info. We were able to analyze what aspects of the app they did well and what the user reviews liked / disliked about their app. Other service dog applications were accused of being falsified, leaving us a great gap in the market. A big issue NAGDU Guide & Service Dog Info faced was interface appearance. There was no graphical influence to their sitemap which allowed us a chance enhance our app in that area. Also, PWAP is an app specifically for veterans, therefore, our audience is very select allowing us to focus on catering to them and them only.

Design

We used yellows and blues to achieve feasibility within a patriotic envirnment. We did not want to overtly use red, white and blue. We found that yellow paired well with the brighter blues and sustained a professional stance. Our primary audience is above the age of 35 years, hence big fonts and icons designed to increase accessibilty and decrease the rate of errors.
