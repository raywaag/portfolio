About

At P & C Casting, users can write reviews for actors, search fo the next star, post casting calls and create profiles with biographies, ratings, past achievments and photo galleries.


We fanaticize about Ryan Reynolds, Sandra Bullock and those alike but a quick look on the media will show you that the world is just as fascinated with the furry, scaley and feathery infamous celebrities. P & C Casting is for them. We are here to enrich the discovery of newly found animal talents and ease the process of talent-finding for the agents.


Animal actors, stretching internationally, who are in need of work desiring to display their profiles, achievements and talents. Humans are easy but animals are the future. Find the next Airbud, Lassie or Trigger with P & C Casting. This neck of the woods is for the geniuses on and off the screen. Talent agents can find their next big star while the actors can promote themselves with confidence.

The Challenge

  • Back-end server database organization. With actors and job postings impending, there will need to be a database to store incoming information.
  • Security. Clearance of job posting fraudulence needs to be immediate. Other sites post jobs that have yet to be verified. This is unsafe and actors should not face the possibility of their information being stolen or ending up in the wrong hands.
  • Features features features. The P & C site comes with a lot of capabilities. These include result filtering, review posting, contact forms and resume submissions.
  • RWD - Responsive Web Design. P & C is predominantly a desktop interface. While we chose not to design mobile-first due to all of the back-end components P & C needed to be mobilized for smartphone access.

Design

P & C's design is influenced by the film and cinema industry - streaming gold, silver and black. The gold and yellows are accetned through out the site to represent significance while black, silver and white made for a distinguishable, professional enterprise. We wanted to limit user clicks to save time and retention which lead to our landing page hosting much of the site's content from current stars to casting calls.

Development Factors

  • Account creation. Actors are able to create profiles, show off their previous works and post a gallery of their best angles. From a back-end prospective, this means user databse table is needed to store the actor's accounts, log them in and out as well as enable new users to join the party.
  • Talent Board filtering. When viewing the talent board, a user may be visiting just for dogs or exotic animals or feathery friends. Simple Javascript allows the user to filter the talent board results.
  • Actor contact. P & C visitors may get in contact with an actor by sendning them a message with their return address. This contact form is located on the actor's profile.
  • Review writing. Users can write actor reviews that immediately post to the actor's accounts live via PHP and MySQL.
  • Resume Submissions. P & C features casting call postings which actors can view, read into and submit their resume if desired.
