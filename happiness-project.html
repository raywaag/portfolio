The Happiness Project



WHAT

With a mission to heal, distract and get a few gnarly laughs, The Happiness Project is an atmosphere to find humor, journaling, stories, tips and distractions.

WHY

I’ve personally payed debt to my demons and wish for others to seek peace in those times. My vision was an environment where people can comfortably exist without judegment or toxic loopholes like social media. Play games, journal, talk to others, seek help and find comfort. That’s what The Happiness Project is about.

WHO

We have all had nights, days, weeks or months that drown us in darkness. The Happiness Project is for those who are unable to itch or cope their way to contentment.




My Roles

Idea Catalyst, Information Architect, Content Creator, UX Copywriter, Interface Designer, Front-End Developer & Back-End Programmer.
Alright, since it was my own project, I did everything.



Workflow

Design

This little guy was one of the first server-side sites I had developed. My design background gave me an advantage when masterplanning the site layout and IA of the content. The real challenges were confronted when it came to server-side responsibilites.




Develop

The Happiness Project was developed using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP and MYSQL with opportunities like email sending, story writing & publishing, profile and account creation as well as journaling and gaming.
