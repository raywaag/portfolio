ShutterSpeed

What
Shutterspeed efficiently connects clients and photographers within location, price range and photographic genre. With Shutterspeed, photographers and profile themselves, showcase work, present testimonials and connects with clients.
Similarly, clients can post jobs, search and find photographers with their area and budget as well as have profiles and contact photographers.
Why
Freelance photographers, like many small business entrepreneurs, gave the obstacles of promotion and advertising while also developing a brand for themselves, connecting with their clients efficiently and doing so without fraudulent data. Photographers often have to rely on third-party applications such as Facebook or Instagram to connect with potential clients and showcase their work. This gives an unfair advantage to the more “popular” photographers, leaving other very skilled photographers in the dark. hutterspeed treats everyone equally and is not based on ratings or popularity.
Clients also may have a difficult time finding a photographer that suits them and their specs especially if they are not social media users. Word of mouth and a good SEO website is all they have in their toolbox.
Who
Clients in need of photographers who are seeking to search with ease, security and efficiency as well as within budget and location.
Likewise, photographers whom are growing or promoting their business, seeking clientele and have the desire to do so with security and assurance.

Workflow

Research

ShutterSpeed's market competitors include Shootster, Care.com, SnapShot. Think alouds were executed during Hierarchical Task Analyses. Through this we found key ways to decrease error rates and time on task and increase enjoyment and retention rates were to exihibit less verbaige and more graphics, simple, clear pathays per clients and photographers and and to provide immediate user feedback. Other fixes include proper functionality (accurate code maps), large mobile icons, and detailed descriptions to avoid misinformation.

Design

ShutterSpeed's color palette was influenced by both Shootster and Care.com to encompass the neighborly feel with professionalism of a workplace. The interface is simple and clean to fashion minimalism and navigability. The landing page is similar to Shootster's while the split road in the upper section of the home is similar to that of Care.com.
