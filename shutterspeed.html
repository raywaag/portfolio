ShutterSpeed

Roles: Project Manager, UX Research, Wireframes, IA, Mock-ups and Interaction Design

ABOUT

A website to centralize connections between photographers and clients with efficiency, security and ease. Designed to solve the issues in searching for photographers by clients and booking sessions by photographers. The site disables the need of third-parties and essentially simplifies conception of client-photographer communication.

THE PROBLEM

Freelance photographers wear many hats. Branding, Marketing, Photographing and Advertising to name a few. This enables some of the greatest photographers to be over- shadowed by other, mediocre photographers strictly due to popularity. Security. Freelancers face fraudulent clients far too often and ShutterSpeed will enforce the proper firewall and validation required to dismiss fraud.

Likewise, clients struggle to find reputable photographers, locally and within their budget. Without having third-party applications that showcase photography (ex. Instagram), or knowing a guy who knows a guy, clients have a difficult time hiring. ShutterSpeed with grant the clients security just as for photographers as well.
